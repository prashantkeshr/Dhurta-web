Privacy & Security

Ghost Mode

In-memory sessions — nothing written to disk, gone the moment the tab closes.

Anti-Fingerprint

Noise on canvas, audio and WebGL reads. Off by default, yours to switch on.

WebRTC Block

Stops pages resolving your real IP behind a VPN or proxy.

Built-in VPN

SOCKS5 routing with a country selector, applied across every session.

Auto-Clean

Wipes session residue on exit automatically.

Data Hub

Local-only SQLite view of history, bookmarks and downloads.

Connectivity & Extensibility

Connect

Pair two Dhurta windows with a 6-digit code, no relay server involved.

Extensions

Load unpacked Chrome extensions directly — no store gate.

Picture-in-Picture

Pop any video or page into a floating, always-on-top window.

Speed Dial
